Empowering Communities in Ethiopia

Hayimi Movement is committed to education, health, and humanitarian support for local communities.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of local leadership, cultural preservation, and inclusive development. Hayimi Movement is a grassroots initiative empowering communities through education, healthcare, and humanitarian support.

What We Do

Education

Supporting local schools, providing scholarships, and equipping students with resources for success.

Healthcare

Providing medical outreach, health education, and essential supplies to underserved communities.

Humanitarian Aid

Delivering food, clothing, and emergency support to internally displaced people and vulnerable families.
