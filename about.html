About Hayimi Movement

We are a grassroots initiative in Ethiopia, empowering communities through education, health, and humanitarian support.

Our Vision

Hayimi Movement envisions a society where local leadership, culture, and inclusive development strengthen communities to thrive sustainably.

Our Values

Empowerment

We support local initiatives and community leaders to create sustainable change.

Integrity

All our programs are transparent, accountable, and ethical.

Inclusivity

We believe every community member deserves equal opportunity and support.
