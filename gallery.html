Hawassa

Hawassa is a vibrant city in southern Ethiopia, known for Lake Hawassa and its breathtaking scenery. It is a hub for education, trade, and cultural exchange. The HI-ME Movement has carried out multiple projects here, supporting schools, providing healthcare outreach, and building sustainable livelihoods for local communities.

Mekele

Mekele, the capital of Tigray region, is a city of resilience and heritage. The HI-ME Movement’s activities in Mekele focus on humanitarian aid, child support, and women’s empowerment programs designed to bring hope and sustainable opportunities to the people of this historic city.

Humera

Humera, located in northwestern Ethiopia, has endured many challenges but remains a community of incredible strength. HI-ME is working to deliver humanitarian assistance, education programs, and health services to help the people of Humera recover and thrive.

Gerji Shoeshine Boy

In Gerji, Addis Ababa, a young boy making a living by shining shoes inspired us. His story reflects the resilience and determination of many Ethiopian youths who strive for a better future. HI-ME Movement’s outreach programs aim to provide education and vocational training to empower children like him.

Virginia

Virginia, USA, is home to a supportive diaspora community that partners with the HI-ME Movement to strengthen connections and resources for projects in Ethiopia. Events, fundraisers, and cultural exchanges held in Virginia help sustain the mission of empowering local communities back home.
